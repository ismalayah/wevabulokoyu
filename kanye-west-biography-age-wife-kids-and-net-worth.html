Kanye West: Biography, Age, Wife, Kids, and Net Worth

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Kanye West is a renowned American rapper, songwriter, performer, record producer, fashion designer, and entrepreneur.

Kanye West: Profile summary

Kanye West: Biography

Kanye West, born on 8th June 1977, hails from a well-settled family in Atlanta, Georgia, United States. He is an American with Christian beliefs, and his early education was at Polaris School.

West showed an interest in drawing and music from a young age and pursued his passion by creating musical compositions from seventh grade. Despite briefly attending college, he left to chase his musical aspirations.

Kanye West: Family, Girlfriend & Relationships

Kanye West is the son of Ray West, a photojournalist, and Donda West, an English professor. He has half-siblings and cousins. He married Kim Kardashian in 2014 and the couple has four children.

Kanye West: Early Career Life

West’s artistic talents were evident early in life, writing poetry and rapping from a young age. His musical journey began in his teenage years, supported by his mentor No I.D. He pursued painting and English studies briefly in college before fully dedicating himself to music.

Kanye West: Music Career Life Summary

  • 1996–2002: Early work and Roc-A-Fella Records
  • 2003–2006: The College Dropout and Late Registration
  • 2007–2009: Graduation, 808s and Heartbreak, and VMAs controversy
  • 2010–2012: My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy and collaborations
  • 2013–2015: Yeezus and Adidas collaboration
  • 2016–2017: The Life of Pablo and tour cancellation
  • 2017–2019: Ye and the Wyoming Sessions
  • 2019–present: Jesus Is King and Donda

Kanye West: Net Worth 2022

As of 2022, Kanye West’s estimated net worth is 1.8 billion USD.
