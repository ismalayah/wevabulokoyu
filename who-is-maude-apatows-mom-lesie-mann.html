Who is Maude Apatow's mom Leslie Mann?

July 2024 Â· 2 minute read

THE BREAKOUT Euphoria star, Maude Apatow, has some acting genes in her family.

Apatow's mom, Leslie Mann, made her television debut in 1996 at just 24, the same age Apatow is now.

Who is Maude Apatow?

Maude Apatow rose to fame in 2019 when she was cast in the HBO hit TV show Euphoria.

Apatow plays Lexi Howard who has been getting more airtime in season two, which currently airs on Sunday nights.

Euphoria creator Sam Levinson wrote the role of Lexi specifically for Apatow after working with her on the film Assassination Nation in 2018.

Apatow stars alongside actress Sydney Sweeney who plays her older sister, Cassie Howard, on Euphoria.

Who is Leslie Mann?

Leslie Mann, 49, is an actress and the mother of two daughters, Maude Apatow and Iris Apatow.

Mann has been married to director, screenwriter, and producer Judd Apatow since 1997.

Mann got her start making commercials at the age of 18 and later made appearances in TV shows including Freaks and Geeks and The Cable Guy.

She co-starred in Judd Apatow's movies Knocked Up and This is 40, as well as co-starring in The 40-Year-Old-Virgin.

Have Apatow and Mann co-acted?

In 2007 at the age of 9, Apatow and her sister made their film debut in Judd Apatow's Knocked Up, playing the daughters of their real-life mother, Leslie Mann.

In 2009, she played Mann's daughter once again in Funny People.

In 2012, she did the same for This is 40.

In 2016, Apatow acted in her first movie that didn't involve her mother or her father.

Apatow and Mann also starred side by side in a Jergens lotion commercial where they played themselves.
