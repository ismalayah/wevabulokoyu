Michelle Yeoh is one of the successful and world-wide famous actresses. The prestigious award-winning Yeoh has portrayed her role in martial art film Crouching Tiger as Yu Shu Lein, comedy-drama film Crazy Rich Asians as Eleanor Young, and on the CBS All Access series Star Trek: Discovery.

The actress was born as Michelle Yeoh Choo-Kheng on August 6, 1963, in Ipoh, Malaysia. She was born to a Malaysian Chinese family Janet Yeoh (mother) and Yeoh Kian Teik (father). Her father was a lawyer and a Malaysian Chinese Association (MCA), politician.  

As per academic details, Michelle majored in ballet from the Royal Academy of Dance located in London. She also received her B.A degree in creative arts.
