William John Neeson professionally known as Liam Neeson is an Irish actor, voiceover artist and former stage performer. As of 2023, Liam Neeson’s net worth is $150 million. He accumulated his net worth through a professional acting career in film and television projects and added a handsome amount through performing in theatre and property investments.

Liam rose to prominence playing the main role as Oskar Schindler in Steven Spielberg’s directional and produced an American epic historical drama entitled Schindler’s List (1993), which received nomination at British Academy Film Award, Golden Globe Award, Academy Award and won Chicago Film Critics Association Award for Best Actor in 1993.

His name was placed on the seventh spot on an Irish daily broadsheet newspaper named The Irish Times published list of Ireland’s top 50 Greatest Film Actors.
